Shetkari Karjmafi Yojana 2026 — Detailed Guide

The flagship scheme in the app is the Maharashtra Shetkari Karjmafi Yojana 2026. Under this scheme, eligible farmers can have their short-term crop loans of up to ₹2 lakh waived. The amount is transferred directly to the farmer's bank account via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ Indian citizen farmer with land registered in their name
  • ✅ Account in a nationalised bank linked to Aadhaar
  • ✅ Crop loan taken before 31 March 2025
  • ✅ Family annual income below ₹2 lakh
  • ✅ Aadhaar number linked to bank account

Required Documents

🪪 Aadhaar Card
📄 7/12 Utara (Satbara)
🏦 Bank Passbook
📋 Loan Account Details
🪪 Ration Card
🗳️ Voter ID
📷 2 Passport Photos

How to Apply for Karjmafi 2026

1

Visit the Official Portal

Go to mjpsky.maharashtra.gov.in — the app opens this directly for you.

2

Click "कर्जमाफी अर्ज"

Select the Karjmafi application option on the home page.

3

Enter Aadhaar & Mobile

Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and registered mobile number.

4

Register via OTP

Verify with the OTP sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.

5

Upload Documents

Upload clear photos of your 7/12, bank passbook and Aadhaar.

6

Submit & Keep Receipt

Submit the form and save your application receipt number for tracking.

⚠️ Important: Helpline for Karjmafi: 1800-180-1551 (toll free). If you face issues with the portal, call this number or visit your nearest CSC (Common Service Centre).

How to Check PM-KISAN Status

  1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in
  2. Click on "Beneficiary Status"
  3. Enter your Aadhaar number
  4. Verify with OTP
  5. View all your installment history

⚠️ eKYC is Mandatory for PM-KISAN: If you have not completed eKYC, your next installment will be withheld. Complete eKYC at pmkisan.gov.in or your nearest CSC centre immediately.

PMFBY Crop Insurance — How to File a Claim

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) provides comprehensive crop insurance at very low premium rates. Farmers pay only 1.5% for Rabi crops and 2% for Kharif crops — the government pays the rest.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) — 4% Interest Loan

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme provides farmers, animal husbandry workers, and fish farmers with revolving credit up to ₹3 lakh at just 4% annual interest (after government interest subsidy). Funds can be withdrawn from any ATM as needed.

  • Who is eligible: All farmers, PM-KISAN beneficiaries get priority. Now extended to fishers and livestock farmers.
  • Where to apply: SBI, PNB, or any cooperative bank nearest to you.
  • Timeline: KCC issued within 14 days of complete document submission.
  • 2026 Target: 4 crore new KCC cards to be issued this year.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Setkari Karjmafi Yojana 2026 and how much loan is waived? +
Shetkari Karjmafi Yojana 2026 is Maharashtra's farmer loan waiver scheme. Eligible farmers can have short-term crop loans of up to ₹2 lakh waived. The waiver amount is transferred directly to the farmer's bank account via DBT. Applications are accepted at mjpsky.maharashtra.gov.in. Helpline: 1800-180-1551.
How to check PM-KISAN 16th installment status? +
Visit pmkisan.gov.in → Click "Beneficiary Status" → Enter your Aadhaar number → Verify OTP → See your installment history. If your installment is not received, check if your eKYC is complete. Incomplete eKYC is the most common reason for withheld payments. The app guides you through this step by step in Marathi.
How to file a PMFBY crop insurance claim? +
If your crop is damaged, you must report within 72 hours. Visit pmfby.gov.in, call the PMFBY helpline 14447, or inform your bank/insurance company. You will need your land records, Aadhaar, and photos/video of the crop damage. Claims are settled within 15 days of approval via DBT.
Can I get both PM-KISAN and Namo Shetkari benefits? +
Yes! If you are a registered PM-KISAN beneficiary in Maharashtra, you automatically qualify for Namo Shetkari Mahasanman Nidhi as well. You receive ₹6,000/year from PM-KISAN (central) + ₹6,000/year from Namo Shetkari (state) = ₹12,000/year total. No separate registration is required for Namo Shetkari if you are already in PM-KISAN.
What is the subsidy for drip irrigation under PMKSY? +
Under PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana), farmers receive 55% subsidy on drip irrigation and sprinkler irrigation systems. SC/ST farmers receive 70% subsidy. Solar pumps attract 75% subsidy. Apply at pmksy.gov.in or at your nearest Krushi Karyalay (agriculture office).
Is the Setkari Karjmafi app affiliated with the Maharashtra government? +
No. The Setkari Karjmafi Krushi Yojana app is an independent, unofficial guide app developed by NextInt Inc. It provides scheme information and opens official government portals inside the app, but is NOT affiliated with the Government of Maharashtra or the Government of India. All scheme applications must be completed on the official government websites.
How to apply for Kisan Credit Card (KCC)? +
Visit your nearest SBI, Punjab National Bank, or cooperative bank. Fill the KCC application form and submit: Aadhaar card, 7/12 land record, bank account details, and 2 passport photos. PM-KISAN beneficiaries get priority processing. KCC is issued within 14 working days. You can then withdraw from ATMs as needed. Annual interest is only 4% after government subsidy.
