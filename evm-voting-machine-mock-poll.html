What is the EVM Voting Machine Mock Poll App?

The EVM Voting Machine – Mock Poll app by NextInt Inc brings the experience of India's Electronic Voting Machine to your Android smartphone. Whether you're a teacher running a classroom demo, a society secretary managing a colony election, or a student organising a college vote — this app gives you a realistic, fair, and easy-to-use voting setup without any expensive equipment.

The app simulates the look, feel, and workflow of a real EVM — complete with candidate buttons, a beep on every vote, password-protected results, and live bar chart tallies. It even supports a two-phone Bluetooth mode where one phone acts as the EVM and another as the Controller.

Three Voting Modes Explained

The app gives you three flexible modes to match any election setup — from a single-phone classroom demo to a two-phone authenticated polling booth simulation:

ModeHow It WorksBest For
🗳️ Direct Voting One phone. Set up candidates, hand it to each voter in turn. Results unlocked by password at the end. Small classrooms, quick group votes, informal polls
📡 EVM + Controller Two phones paired via Bluetooth. One is the EVM (voting device) — passed to each voter. The other is the Controller — stays with the election officer. Society elections, school elections, panchayat demo, realistic setups
🎮 Controller Only Use a second phone purely as the remote controller to manage an existing EVM session — start, stop, next voter, view results. When you want physical separation between EVM and election officer

How to Conduct a Mock Election — Step by Step

Running your first election takes less than 5 minutes. Here's how:

1

Install & Open the App

Download from Google Play Store. Open the app and select your preferred voting mode (Direct / EVM+Controller).

2

Add Candidates

Enter each candidate's name, select a symbol from 60+ emoji options or pick a photo from the gallery. Add up to 12 candidates.

3

Set a Result Password

Choose a secure password — only you (the election officer) will use this to unlock the results after voting ends.

4

Start Voting

Hand the EVM phone to each voter in turn. They press the button next to their chosen candidate — a beep confirms the vote.

5

Close Voting & View Results

Enter your password to stop voting. See live bar chart results, winner announcement, and share results via WhatsApp or SMS.
