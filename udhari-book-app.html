What is Udhari Book and Who Is It For?

Udhari Book is a free Android app built specifically for Indian shopkeepers, traders, and small business owners who manage daily cash, customer credit, and business calculations. Whether you run a kirana store, a cloth shop, a medical store, or offer any service — this app replaces your paper khata with a simple, private digital system.

Every feature is designed for the Indian market: Indian currency denominations, GST slab calculations with CGST & SGST split, reminders in Hindi, Marathi and English, and amounts displayed in the Indian lakh/crore format.

🔒 100% Private: All your data stays on your phone. No account, no login, no internet required. Nothing is uploaded to any server — ever.

GST Calculator — Add or Extract GST Instantly

The built-in GST calculator is one of the most useful features for Indian traders. You can both add GST to a base price or extract GST from an inclusive price — with the automatic CGST & SGST breakdown shown clearly.

💡 Tip: Use the "Extract GST" mode when a customer gives you an MRP-inclusive price and you need to find the original base price and tax amount separately.

How to Use Udhari Book — Step by Step

1

Download & Open the App

Install Udhari Book from Play Store. No signup or login required — it opens directly to your home screen.

2

Set Your Business Name

Go to Settings, enter your shop name and owner name. This appears on all reports and shared messages.

3

Add Customer Entries (Udhari)

Tap Udhari Book → Add Customer. Record how much you gave (Diya) or received (Liya). Balance updates automatically.

4

Use Cash Counter at Day End

Count all notes and coins using the Cash Counter. The app totals in lakhs/crores and shows the amount in words.

5

Fill Daily Register

Record your Opening balance, Sales, and Expenses. Closing balance is calculated automatically. Share the report in one tap.

6

Send Payment Reminders

Select a customer with pending balance → Send Reminder → choose WhatsApp or SMS → select Hindi, Marathi or English.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Udhari Book app? +
Udhari Book is a free Android business app for Indian shopkeepers and traders. It includes a cash counter for all Indian denominations, a daily register, a customer credit (udhari) ledger, and a business calculator with GST support — all without any login or internet connection.
Is the app free? Are there any hidden charges? +
Yes, the app is completely free to download and use. There are no premium plans, no subscription fees, and no in-app purchases. All features are available for free.
Can I use the app without internet? +
Yes. Udhari Book works completely offline. All data is stored on your device. You only need internet if you want to share reports or reminders via WhatsApp or SMS.
Which GST slabs are supported in the calculator? +
The GST calculator supports all standard Indian tax slabs — 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. It shows the CGST and SGST split automatically. You can both add GST to a base price or extract GST from an inclusive MRP price.
Can I send payment reminders in Hindi or Marathi? +
Yes. You can send payment reminders to customers via WhatsApp or SMS in Hindi, Marathi, or English. You can also customise the reminder message and set a default reminder language in the app Settings.
Does the app store my data on any server? +
No. The Udhari Book app does NOT collect, process, or store any personal or business data on any server. All your customer entries, cash counts, and register data stay only on your device.
Is this app available on iPhone (iOS)? +
Currently Udhari Book is available only on Android via the Google Play Store. An iOS version has not been announced yet.
