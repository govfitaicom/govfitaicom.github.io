Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released Advertisement No. 04/2026 for CET Phase-II Group C Recruitment 2026. Online applications are invited from candidates who have qualified CET Group C Advt. No. 01/2025. This is a golden opportunity for 10th/12th pass and graduate candidates seeking government jobs in Haryana with no application fee. Applications can be submitted from 9 February 2026 to 23 February 2026 (11:59 PM) through the official portal https://adv042026.hryssc.com. The written examination will be OMR-based (100 MCQs, 100 marks, 1 hour 45 minutes) with 50% qualifying marks for General and 40% for reserved categories.
