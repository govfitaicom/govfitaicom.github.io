Recruitment Overview

Lalit Narayan Mithila University invites applications for the CET-BED 2025 entrance exam, offering regular, distance, and Shiksha Shastri courses. The online registration process commenced on April 4, 2025, and will conclude on May 2, 2025. Aspirants can submit their applications for the 2-year B.Ed program within this timeframe. It is essential to review the eligibility criteria and guidelines for the Bihar CET B.Ed online form 2025 to ensure a seamless application process. For the Shiksha Shastri (B.Ed. in Sanskrit) course, candidates need a Bachelor's degree with Sanskrit as a primary subject and a Master's in Sanskrit, or an Acharya in traditional Sanskrit Shastra with 50% marks, or a Shastri degree with 50% marks and a completed 2-year Acharya programme.
