Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does WA Status Saver work? +
Open WhatsApp and view your contacts' statuses first. Then open WA Status Saver — the statuses you just viewed will appear in the app. Tap the save button on any photo or video to download it to your gallery. The app reads from WhatsApp's local status folder on your device.
Why don't I see any statuses in the app? +
Make sure you have opened WhatsApp and actually viewed (tapped on) your contacts' statuses first. WhatsApp only saves statuses locally after they are viewed. Also ensure you have granted storage permission to the WA Status Saver app in your phone settings.
Does it work with WhatsApp Business? +
Yes! WA Status Saver supports both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. Statuses from both accounts appear together in the app for easy access.
Where are saved statuses stored on my phone? +
Saved statuses are stored in your phone's gallery/photos app. You can find them in your device storage under Pictures or DCIM folder. They are accessible just like any other photo or video you save to your gallery.
Is the app free? Are there any hidden charges? +
Yes, the app is completely free. No premium plan, no subscription, no in-app purchases. All features — save, share, repost, add caption, delete — are free for everyone.
Is it available on iPhone (iOS)? +
Currently WA Status Saver is available only on Android via Google Play Store. An iOS version has not been announced yet.
