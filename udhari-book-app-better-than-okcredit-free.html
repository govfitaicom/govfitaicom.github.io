📱Udhari Book App क्या है? — What is Udhari Book?

Udhari Book — Cash & GST Calc is a free Android app made for Indian shopkeepers, kirana store owners, traders, and small business owners. It brings together four powerful tools in one place — a cash counter, a daily register, a customer credit (udhari) ledger, and a business calculator with GST.

Unlike OkCredit, Khatabook, or Vyapar — Udhari Book requires no login, no account creation, and no internet connection. All your data is stored only on your phone. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your business data is 100% private.

Whether you run a kirana store, vegetable shop, cloth store, mobile shop, or any small business — Udhari Book is designed to replace your physical bahi khata and calculator in one simple app.

✅ Key USP: Udhari Book is the only free offline app that combines Cash Counter (all Indian notes/coins) + Udhar Ledger + Daily Register + GST Calculator — with WhatsApp reminders in Hindi, Marathi & English. No login. No subscription. No data upload.

🔒Privacy & Security — Why No Login Matters

Most business apps — OkCredit, Khatabook, Vyapar — sync your customer data, transaction history, and business financials to their cloud servers. This means:

  • Your customer names and phone numbers are stored on their servers
  • Your business revenue data is accessible to third parties
  • If their server is breached, your data is at risk
  • They can use your data for advertising or analytics

Udhari Book is different:

  • ✅ No login or account — nothing tied to your identity
  • ✅ All data stored only on your device — not on any server
  • ✅ No data uploaded — zero server communication for your business data
  • ✅ Works 100% offline — internet is never needed

🔐 For Indian Small Business Owners: Your customer list and daily cash figures are sensitive business data. With Udhari Book, that data never leaves your phone. This is a fundamentally different — and safer — approach to business data privacy.
