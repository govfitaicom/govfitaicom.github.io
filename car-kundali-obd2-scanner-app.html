How to Get Started — Step by Step

You'll be reading live engine data in under 5 minutes. Here's how:

1

Buy a Bluetooth ELM327 OBD2 Adapter

Available on Amazon India or Flipkart for ₹300–₹800. Search "ELM327 Bluetooth OBD2 adapter". Make sure to get a Bluetooth version, not Wi-Fi.

2

Plug the Adapter into Your Car's OBD2 Port

Locate the OBD2 port — usually under the dashboard on the driver's side. Plug in the ELM327 adapter. Turn your car's ignition to ON (engine doesn't need to start for most scans).

3

Download Car Kundali from Play Store

Search "Car Kundali" on Google Play Store, or click the download button on this page. Install and open the app on your Android phone (Android 6.0 or above).

4

Pair the Adapter via Bluetooth

Go to your phone's Bluetooth settings and pair with the ELM327 device (usually named "OBDII", "ELM327", or "V-LINK"). Then return to Car Kundali and tap Connect.

5

Start Scanning & Monitoring

Once connected, read fault codes, watch live gauges, check your health score, or start a trip. All data updates in real time directly from your vehicle's ECU.

⚠️ Important: Clearing DTCs (fault codes) does not fix the underlying mechanical problem — it only resets the warning light. Always address the root cause. If the Check Engine Light returns after clearing, visit a qualified mechanic.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Car Kundali and how does it work? +
Car Kundali is a free Android OBD2 scanner app that connects to your car's ECU via a Bluetooth ELM327 adapter. It reads live sensor data, diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs), vehicle health score, trip computer data, and performance metrics directly from your vehicle's on-board computer in real time.
Which ELM327 adapter should I buy? +
Any standard Bluetooth ELM327 OBD2 adapter works. Search "ELM327 Bluetooth OBD2" on Amazon India or Flipkart — prices range from ₹300 to ₹800. Always buy the Bluetooth version (not Wi-Fi). Avoid extremely cheap "v1.5 clone" adapters as they can have firmware bugs. A ₹400–₹600 adapter typically works well.
Which Indian car brands does Car Kundali support? +
Car Kundali works with Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata, Mahindra, Honda, Toyota, Kia, Renault, Nissan, Ford, Volkswagen, Skoda, MG, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, and most other brands. Any car with a standard OBD2 port (petrol cars after 2000, diesel after 2004) should be compatible.
Can Car Kundali clear the Check Engine Light? +
Yes. Car Kundali can read and clear Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs), which resets the Check Engine Light (CEL). However, if the underlying mechanical issue is not fixed, the CEL will return after a few drive cycles. Always diagnose and repair the root cause first.
Does the app work on bikes / motorcycles? +
Car Kundali is primarily designed for OBD2-compliant cars and SUVs. Some motorcycles with OBD2 ports (typically BS6/Euro5 compliant bikes from 2020 onwards) may work, but compatibility varies by manufacturer and not all bike parameters will be available.
Is Car Kundali free? Are there in-app purchases? +
Yes, Car Kundali is completely free to download and use. There are no subscriptions, no hidden charges, and no locked features. All diagnostic, live data, trip computer, performance, and advanced terminal features are available at no cost. You only need to purchase a Bluetooth ELM327 adapter separately.
What Android version is required? +
Car Kundali requires Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or above. This covers the vast majority of Android smartphones currently in use in India, including budget phones from 2017 onwards.
