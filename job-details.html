📝 How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for this job:

  1. Click on the "Apply Now" button in the sidebar to go to the official government portal
  2. Register or login on the official recruitment website using your email and mobile number
  3. Fill the application form carefully with your personal, educational and category details
  4. Upload required documents: recent passport photo, signature, educational certificates, caste/category certificate (if applicable), and valid ID proof
  5. Pay the application fee online (if applicable) via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, or UPI. SC/ST/PWD/Ex-Servicemen candidates are usually exempted
  6. Review the filled form before final submission to avoid errors
  7. Submit the form and save/print the confirmation page and application number for future reference

⚠️ Important: Verify all eligibility criteria (age, education, category) before applying. Keep scanned copies of documents ready in specified format (JPG/PDF, file size limits).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there any application fee for this recruitment?

A: Application fees vary by category and organization. SC/ST/PWD/Women (in many state recruitments) and Ex-Servicemen candidates are usually exempted. Check the official notification linked on this page for exact fee details.

Q: Can I edit my application after submission?

A: Most government portals open a correction window 1–2 weeks after the initial application period closes. You can update basic details during this window. Final submission after correction is binding — no further changes are permitted.

Q: What documents are required at the time of application?

A: Commonly required documents include: recent passport-size photograph (JPG, 20–50 KB), scanned signature (JPG, 10–20 KB), Class 10 certificate for date-of-birth proof, educational qualification certificates, caste/category certificate (for SC/ST/OBC/EWS), Aadhaar card or valid government ID, and disability certificate (if applicable).

Q: When will the exam/selection be conducted?

A: Exam dates are announced separately via official admit card notifications after the application deadline. Bookmark the official website and check GovFitAI regularly for updates on this recruitment.

Q: Is there negative marking?

A: Negative marking rules depend on the specific exam. Most competitive government exams have 0.25 or 0.50 marks deducted per wrong answer. Some paper-based descriptive tests have no negative marking. Refer to the official notification for exact rules.

Q: What is the selection process?

A: Selection process typically includes: Written/Online Examination → Physical/Skill Test (if applicable) → Document Verification → Medical Examination → Final Merit List. Some posts may include an interview round. Check the official notification for the complete selection process of this recruitment.
