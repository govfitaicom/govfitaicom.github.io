What is AgriStack and Why Does Every Farmer Need to Register?

AgriStack is India's national Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) for agriculture, developed by the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare. At its core is the Farmers' Registry — a unified digital database that links each farmer's identity (via Aadhaar) with their land records.

Once registered, a farmer gets a unique Farmer ID that becomes the gateway to all government agricultural schemes — including PM-KISAN payments, crop insurance (PMFBY), KCC loans, and state-level benefits. In short: no registration = no benefits in the near future.

📢 Important: AgriStack farmer registration is being made mandatory across states. Farmers who register now will have seamless access to future scheme benefits without additional paperwork.

The Problem Farmers Face — And How This App Solves It

Every state has its own AgriStack portal with a different URL. A farmer in Maharashtra needs mhfr.agristack.gov.in, while one in UP needs upfr.agristack.gov.in. Searching for these on Google often leads to:

  • ❌ Fake third-party websites that steal data
  • ❌ Outdated links that no longer work
  • ❌ Confusion between state and central portals
  • ❌ Wrong forms being filled

The Agri Stack All India app solves this by providing a verified, curated directory of all official state portals. Select your state — the correct official government website opens directly inside the app. Simple, safe, instant.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is AgriStack farmer registration? +
AgriStack is India's Digital Public Infrastructure for agriculture. The Farmers' Registry links each farmer's Aadhaar identity with their land records to create a verified Farmer ID used for all government scheme benefits including PM-KISAN, PMFBY, and KCC.
Is AgriStack registration mandatory? +
The Government of India is progressively making AgriStack registration mandatory for receiving agricultural scheme benefits. States like Maharashtra, Bihar, and UP have already begun linking scheme eligibility to Farmer Registry data. It is strongly advised to register as early as possible.
How is this app different from the official AgriStack website? +
This is an independent launcher app, not affiliated with the government. It simply opens the correct official state AgriStack portals inside the app — saving farmers the trouble of finding the right URL. All actual registration happens on the official government websites.
Does the app store my Aadhaar or land data? +
No. The Agri Stack All India app does NOT collect, process, or store any personal data including Aadhaar numbers, land records, or bank details. All data is entered and processed directly on the official government portals.
My state is not in the list — what do I do? +
AgriStack portals are being launched state by state. If your state is not yet listed, you can visit agristack.gov.in directly or wait for the app to be updated when your state portal goes live. The developer regularly updates the app as new state portals are announced.
